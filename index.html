About Me

I am Chetan Chandrashekar Gaonkar, a visual creator with skills in photography, cinematography, and basic design. I am basically from a village called Gund, near Dandeli. Growing up in a place surrounded by forests and greenery naturally made me curious about details, colours, and moments around me.

That environment built the foundation of my creativity and pushed me towards capturing things I felt connected to. Being close to nature helped me develop a habit of observing — noticing light, shadows, textures, and the quiet stories unfolding around me.

Where My Creative Journey Began

Before I even knew what photography was, I found myself quietly observing how light hits leaves, how shadows move, how colours change after rain, and how nature tells its own story without words.

I was drawn to small, often unnoticed moments — textures on tree bark, animals moving through the forest, flowing water, and clouds forming patterns in the sky. These moments made me feel something, and I wanted to freeze that feeling.

When I finally got access to a camera, I naturally turned towards nature, landscapes, and wildlife. With every photo, I learned patience, how to understand light, wait for the right moment, choose the right perspective, and tell a story through a single frame. Nature photography became my foundation — it trained my eye, shaped my creativity, and laid the base for everything I do today.

From Curiosity to Creative Craft

I began capturing traditional events, college events, people, moments, and anything visually appealing.

Event photography taught me speed, timing, and confidence — how to work when things are happening fast, how to make people feel comfortable, how to handle pressure.

Later, I was introduced to video editing and designing.

This opened a new world for me:

  • Creating posters in Canva
  • Editing videos in Premiere Pro
  • Designing brochures
  • Being part of the college design committee
  • Learning how visuals can communicate emotion

One step led to another, and soon I was doing freelance shoots, handling clients, and experimenting with both photography and videography.

What I Can Contribute

I bring strong observation skills, quick learning, camera handling knowledge, and a simple but effective understanding of editing and design. I like capturing detailed shots, telling stories through visuals and working in a creative environment.
